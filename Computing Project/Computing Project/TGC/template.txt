		<h2>Laser Quest</h2>
		<br />
			<h3>What is Laser Quest? </h3>
				<p>Laser Quest is the best known and most exhilarating game of Laser tag. 
                	No matter what age you are you will love the adrenaline rush of not 
                    knowing where your opponents are and unleashing a barrage of laser fire in the battle-zone.
                </p>
			<br />
			<h3>Playing the Game</h3>
				<p>To play the game of Laser quest you enter a surreal world where you
                compete in teams or free-for-all. Each player is equipped with laser tag
                equipment and the game is played in a unique labyrinth, with amazing
                features such as custom scenery, catwalks, lighting effects and tense music
                <br />
                Points are Scored when you successfully zap your opponent on one of the
                sensors located on their gun or pack. Be warned if you get zapped then you
                will lose points and get knocked out of the the game for a short period of
                time.
                <br />
                Using the Laser Quest Computer you can create limitless game variations such
                as simple team based games to more complex like base station games.
				</p>
            <h3>What's the Score?</h3>
            	<p>The normal game sessions usually last around 20 minutes and can have up
                to 20 players in the session. Each session includes all of the equipment and
                the briefing on how to play.
                <br />
                At the end your game scores will be displayed on the monitor and a
                scorecard will be printed out for you to keep.</p>
